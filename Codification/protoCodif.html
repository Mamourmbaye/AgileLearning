Module Basics

Codification
The universal codification is divided in 5 parts :

1. One digit, representing article's type.
2. Eight digits automatically incremented, without any sens.
3. Two digits representing the article's major version.
4. One capital lettre representing the article's minor version.
5. One optional W, for articles in Warranty Repair.
